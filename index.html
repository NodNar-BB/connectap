How to Play

Find four groups of four words that share an anatomical connection.

  • Select four tiles you think belong together, then tap Submit.
  • Groups are colour-coded by difficulty — easy to hardest.
  • Watch out for red herrings — words that seem to fit but belong elsewhere.
  • You have 4 mistakes before the game ends.
  • Use Shuffle to rearrange tiles for fresh perspective.
